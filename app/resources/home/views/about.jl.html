What does motregen mean?

Motregen is Dutch for drizzling or soft rain. "Mot" in old Dutch is another word for dust, so, rain as soft as dust, but in the modern day "mot" means moth. So try to imagine "moths flying around like crazy at night in the street lights", like Stav once said.
